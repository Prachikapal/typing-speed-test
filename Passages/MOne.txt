Technology has changed the way people communicate, learn, and work in modern society. In the past, most information was stored in books and libraries, and people had to spend hours searching for the knowledge they needed. Today, with the help of the internet, information is available within seconds. This convenience has made life easier, but it has also created new challenges.

One of the biggest advantages of technology is that it connects people from different parts of the world. Friends, families, and colleagues can communicate instantly through messages, emails, and video calls. Businesses can operate globally, and students can attend online classes without leaving their homes. However, excessive dependence on technology can reduce face-to-face interaction and sometimes affect productivity.
